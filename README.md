## About The Game

**Shine On, Idol!** is a virtual idol card-drawing simulator where players act as producers, building their dream team through draws, upgrades, and collection. Each draw consumes coins, and players can choose between single draws or ten-pull sessions.

The game recreates the joy of gacha games through code‚Äîthe thrill of drawing your favorites, watching them grow, and gradually completing your collection. It's a relaxed but engaging experience that blends collecting, progression, and light strategy.

## ‚ú® Main Features

### Idol Collection System
- **26 Unique Idols** - each with their own portrait, personality, level, and fan count
- **4 Rarity Tiers:** Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
- **Leveling System:** Drawing duplicate idols automatically levels them up and increases their fan count
- **Collection Viewer:** View all your collected idols and track their stats

### Drawing Mechanics
- **Single Draw (10 coins):** Draw one random idol card
- **Ten Draw (100 coins):** Draw 10 cards with a **guaranteed Rare+ card**
- **Smart Duplicate System:** Duplicates automatically level up your existing idols and refund coins based on rarity

### Player-Friendly Design
- **Bankruptcy Protection:** Automatic bonus coins when you can't afford a draw
- **Auto-Save System:** Your progress saves automatically after every draw
- **Real-Time Stats:** Track your coins, total draws, collection count, and total fans

## üéÆ How To Run

- Python 3.8 or higher

### Installation

1. **Install the required library:**
```bash
pip install pillow
```

```bash

```

## üéØ How To Play

### Main Interface Buttons

**Drawing:**
- Click "Single Draw" to draw one idol card (costs 10 coins)
- Click "Ten-Draw" to draw 10 idol cards with guaranteed Rare+ (costs 100 coins)

**Menu Options:**
- Click "View Collection" to see your collected idols and their stats
- Click "Idol Profiles" to view detailed information about idols you've already drawn
- Click "Help" to view in-game instructions and game mechanics
- Click "Reset Data" to start a fresh game
- Click "Save & Exit" to save your progress and close the game

### Gameplay Tips
- Check the Help button in-game for detailed mechanics and duplicate refund rates
- View Idol Profiles to learn more about the idols you've collected
- View your Collection anytime to track your progress
- Ten-draws give you a guaranteed Rare+ idol - perfect for building your collection!
- Don't worry about running out of coins - bankruptcy protection will help you continue playing
